Affects: `meltano.yml` target dataset config, `dbt_project.yml`, `sources.yml`, Dagster `DbtProject` config, `scripts/generate_parquet.py` (default `--dataset` argument), notebooks. | 2026-03-14 | Meltano target method | Changed `target-bigquery` method from `storage_write_api` to `batch_job` | `storage_write_api` holds open gRPC streams per table.
