| 2026-03-11 | BigQuery datasets | Renamed `raw` → `olist_raw`; `analytics` → `olist_analytics` | `raw` is a reserved word in BigQuery standard SQL. | 2026-03-14 | Meltano target method | Changed `target-bigquery` method from `storage_write_api` to `batch_job` | `storage_write_api` holds open gRPC streams per table.
